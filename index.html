How the Bot Works

Our system operates in three critical phases to ensure maximum performance and security.

  1. Connection & Analysis: The bot connects to Solana nodes and scans the blockchain for events, transactions, and new liquidity pool opportunities.
  2. Strategy Execution: Based on the selected strategy (Sniper, MEV, etc.), the bot evaluates the opportunity in microseconds and executes the trade if it meets your risk and profit parameters.
  3. Management & Reporting: Once executed, the bot manages the position (stop-loss, take-profit) and reports every move to you in real-time via your dashboard.
